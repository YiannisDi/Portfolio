About Me

I’m a curious person who loves learning how things work — whether it’s a new framework, a creative workflow, or just a clever way to make something look better on screen. My work often blends design and technology, with an emphasis on clean interfaces and intuitive experiences.

When I’m not working on projects, I enjoy experimenting with small ideas, reading about emerging trends in tech, and sometimes breaking things just to see if I can fix them again. I believe good design tells a story, and good code keeps that story alive.

My process usually starts with a sketch or a simple concept, then evolves into a prototype, and finally becomes something interactive that users can actually touch and feel.
